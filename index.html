About Me

Jaime Gallegos Garcia is known for his over abundant imagination, something that has allowed him to excel in several fields. His interests range from working on graphic design to working with code. As he continues to build his portfolio in these fields, it is clear that both his passions and determination to improve, will drive him to be the best that he can.

Based in Houston Texas, it was here that he was first introduced to coding and design. While attending Westchester Academy he quickly found a home in the art studio and computer rooms. Graduating with honors he ran with these interests and began his studies at Lonestar College, where he studied graphic design. Putting these courses on pause, he later shifted his attention to coding, going so far as to join the coding bootcamp offered by Rice University.
